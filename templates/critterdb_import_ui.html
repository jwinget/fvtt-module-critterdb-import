To get your critter or bestiary from CritterDB:

For a single creature:

  • Click on the down arrow in the upper right of the stat block
  • Select "Export"
  • Select "Export to JSON"
  • Select "CritterDB JSON"
  • Click "Copy to Clipboard"

For an entire bestiary:

  • Open the bestiary
  • Click the three-dots menu in the upper right
  • Select "Export"
  • Select "CritterDB JSON"
  • Click "Copy to Clipboard"

Paste the JSON into the box below and click "Import".
